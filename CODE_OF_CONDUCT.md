# Code Of Conduct

This project welcomes focused, respectful collaboration.

## Expected Behavior

- Be constructive and specific.
- Assume good intent while still naming concrete risks.
- Respect different experience levels, languages, and tooling preferences.
- Keep discussion tied to the project and its users.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Dismissing reports of security, safety, or accessibility concerns without
  review.
- Repeated off-topic disruption.

Maintainers may remove comments, close threads, or limit participation when
behavior makes collaboration unsafe or unproductive.
